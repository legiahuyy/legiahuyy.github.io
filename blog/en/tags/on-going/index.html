As promised, this is the beginning of our solutions for the book Practical Reverse Engineering I had mentioned earlier. The utmost objective is to solve and provide concrete explanations for each topic. The target audiences are believed to: Be familiar with basic mid-level programming language Have at least a fundamental understanding of computer architecture (CPU, RAM, etc.) Be determined to learn and do exercises in the book And please be noticed that all of these solutions here are explained in my own comprehension and experience as there may have been some miscalculations and inaccuracies therein; hence, readers are recommended to review and reproduce these solutions if possible.
