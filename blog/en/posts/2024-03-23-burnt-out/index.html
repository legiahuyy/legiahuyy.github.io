Burnt out

It must have been more than a year and a half since my last post on this site. A lot of things have changed during my absence: I got a job, and it has been pretty good so far, at least. It’s not like there is something wrong with my life at the moment; you just get a little burnt out, but sooner or later, things will get back on track again… or that’s just what other people, and even I, thought. “You don’t know till it happens to you,” and it finally got to me. It was nothing at first, but slowly I started to feel bored with everything. I’ve been trying to learn something new, find a new hobby, play a couple of League matches with friends, but only after a few days, I lose interest again. Whenever I try to do what I usually did for entertainment, now just looking at it makes me sick, and all I did was sit there and do nothing. Maybe I really do need those idling time, or maybe I can’t handle myself being unproductive; you tell me.

All that rantings aside, I also intend to post more about Windows kernel, bootloaders stuff since I did a great research on these subjects, I was gone for too long after all. Hopefully, this awful sensation soon goes away. It feels like crap even sitting here writing this for god-knows-who readers (no offense, though).
